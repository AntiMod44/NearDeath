How to Play

The game consists of preventing enemies from touching the player by dodging and destroying them. There are 3 difficulties, each one except normal difficulty has speed increase on enemies.

Controls


[W/Up Arrow] = Up

[S/Down Arrow] = Down

[A/Left Arrow] = Left

[D/Right Arrow] = Right

[Left-Click] = Shot
